Oringao Weavers



ORINGAO ABACA HANDLOOM WEAVERS




A small barangay called Oringao is located 12 kilometers away from Kabankalan City. The main source of income of the residences there is centered on sugarcane farming. They needed an alternative source of income that is why the women of the Barangay Oringao ventured into loom weaving and manufacturing polyester thread and abaca fiber. Since 2003, their association has been producing hand-woven products as part of their marginal income.
